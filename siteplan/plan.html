ElegantBites Catering – Site Plan

Site Name

ElegantBites Catering

This name reflects the business’s focus on elegant, high-quality catering services for events. It’s easy to remember, professional-sounding, and versatile across different types of events from weddings to corporate luncheons.

Optional domain availability: elegantbitescatering.com

Site Purpose

The website serves as a central hub for customers to explore catering packages, browse menu options, view event galleries, read client testimonials, and submit inquiries or booking requests. It aims to improve the online visibility of the business and streamline the client engagement process.

Scenarios

  • What types of events does ElegantBites Catering serve, and what packages are available?
  • How do I make a booking or send an inquiry about my upcoming event?

Color Scheme

  • Crimson Red (#c0392b): Used for headers, buttons, and key highlights to evoke elegance and appetite.
  • Champagne Beige (#fff8f0): Used as the background color to give a warm, clean look.

Typography

  • Playfair Display: Used for headings to reflect sophistication and class.
  • Roboto: Used for body text for readability and modern appearance.
